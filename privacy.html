Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026 · Last Updated: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Spent: Money Tracker ("Spent", "we", "our", "the app") is a personal finance application designed with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

App Name: Spent: Money Tracker
Developer: Rahul Bhichher
Contact Email: rahulbhichher@gmail.com
Bundle Identifier: com.bhichher.spentmoneytracker

2. Data Storage — Offline-First Architecture

Spent is built on an offline-first architecture. All your financial data — including transactions, accounts, categories, subscriptions, and settings — is stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not operate any servers that collect, store, or process your personal financial data.

Your financial data never leaves your device.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Transaction details (amounts, merchants, dates, notes)
  • Account names or balances
  • Category or subscription information
  • Spending habits, patterns, or personality insights
  • Clipboard contents
  • Biometric data (Face ID / Touch ID / Fingerprint templates)

4. Clipboard Access

Spent includes a "Paste & Log" feature that reads your device clipboard to detect transaction information from copied bank SMS messages, UPI confirmations, or payment receipts. This processing happens entirely on your device. Clipboard data is:

  • Processed locally using on-device parsing
  • Never transmitted to any external server
  • Cleared from the app's memory after detection
  • Only accessed when the app is opened or when you explicitly use the "Paste & Extract" feature

You can disable clipboard auto-detection in the app's Settings.

5. Currency Exchange Rates

When you use the Currency Converter feature, the app fetches live exchange rates from a Cloudflare Workers proxy. This is the only network request the app makes for functional purposes. The request contains:

  • The currency pair being converted (e.g., USD to INR)
  • No personal or financial data is included in this request

Exchange rates are cached locally with a 6-hour TTL, and conversions work offline using cached rates.

6. Third-Party Services

a) Google AdMob

Spent displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share your financial data with Google. You can upgrade to Spent Pro to remove ads.

b) In-App Purchases

Spent Pro subscriptions are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple/Google. We do not have access to your payment information.

c) Notifications

Spent uses local device notifications for reminders and alerts. Push notification tokens may be generated by Firebase Cloud Messaging for Android. No personal financial data is transmitted through notifications.

7. Biometric Authentication

Spent supports Face ID, Touch ID, and Fingerprint authentication to secure app access. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system. Spent never accesses, stores, or transmits biometric templates — it only receives a success/failure response from the OS.

8. Data Security

  • All data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device
  • SHA-256 hashing is used for transaction fingerprinting (duplicate prevention)
  • Sensitive settings are stored using Expo SecureStore (Keychain on iOS, Encrypted SharedPreferences on Android)
  • Biometric authentication adds an additional layer of protection

9. Data Export & Deletion

Export: You can export your data at any time as CSV or PDF from the Reports screen.

Deletion: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data since everything is stored locally. You can also delete individual transactions, accounts, or categories within the app.

10. Children's Privacy

Spent does not knowingly collect personal data from children. The app is intended for users aged 18 and above.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can view, export, modify, or delete any of your data directly within the app.
