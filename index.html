Features

Scroll to explore what makes Spent different.

🔒
01

100% Offline & Private

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your financial life stays yours — completely.

📚
02

Multi-Account Passbooks

Manage Cash, Bank, Savings, and custom accounts. Each passbook gets its own balance, color, and transaction history.

⚡
03

Smart Transaction Entry

Quick-add expenses, income, or transfers with auto-focused amount fields. The fastest way to log what you spend.

📷
04

Receipt Scanner

Point your camera at any receipt or pick one from your gallery. On-device OCR extracts the total, merchant name, date, and line items with a confidence indicator. All processing happens locally — zero cloud calls.

📋
05

Paste & Log

Copy a bank SMS, UPI confirmation, or receipt. Spent scans your clipboard, extracts the transaction, and queues it for one-tap approval. The merchant learning engine remembers your category choices over time. All on-device.

🛡
06

Duplicate Prevention

SHA-256 fingerprinting ensures the same transaction is never logged twice. Smart enough to catch even subtle duplicates.

🎨
07

Custom Categories

Build hierarchical category trees with 24+ icons and 15 color options. Organize both income and expenses exactly how you think about money.

🎯
08

Budget Goals & Alerts

Set monthly spending limits per category or an overall budget. Color-coded progress bars on the Dashboard turn green → orange → red. Get push notifications at 80% and 100% of your limit.

🔄
09

Recurring Transactions

Schedule recurring expenses and income with daily, weekly, or monthly frequency. Supports weekday-only scheduling and auto-backfills missed days when you open the app.

🌱
10

Saving Goals

Create saving goals with a target amount and deadline. Log contributions over time and watch a visual progress bar fill up. Track what you've saved, what remains, and your projected completion pace.

🔄
11

Subscription Tracker

Log recurring subscriptions with billing cycles and inline currency conversion. See your true estimated monthly burn rate and never get surprised by a renewal again.

💱
12

Currency Converter

Real-time exchange rates for 10+ currencies via Cloudflare Workers. Rates are cached with a 6-hour TTL and a 3-tier fallback ensures it works offline. Log multi-currency transactions that record the original amount and exchange rate.

🧮
13

EMI Calculator

Enter loan amount, interest rate, and tenure to instantly calculate your monthly EMI, total interest, and total payment. Expand the full amortization schedule with principal vs. interest breakdown for every month.

💡
14

Spending Personality

Rule-based analytics classifies you as Saver, Balanced, Spender, or Impulse Buyer — with confidence scores and actionable advice.

🔍
15

Pattern Detection

Identifies spending behaviors like Weekend Splurge, Late-Night Shopping, Front-Loaded Spending, and Subscription Trap — each with supporting evidence and recommendations.

🎞
16

Monthly Stories

Instagram-style animated slideshows that recap your financial month with metrics, savings rate, daily averages, and personality-driven narrative insights.

📈
17

Reports & Export

Filter by Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days, or a custom date range. Animated bar charts visualize your spending trend. Export to CSV, PDF, or share a visual chart screenshot.

🔓
18

Biometric Lock

Secure your data with Face ID, Touch ID, or Fingerprint. Your finances stay private even if someone picks up your phone.

🔔
19

Smart Notifications

Daily reminders at 8 AM plus your chosen time, weekly spending insights every Sunday, spending check-ins at 6 PM, subscription renewal alerts one day before, and monthly reports on the 1st. All individually toggleable.

🌈
20

Theming

Light, Dark, and System-auto themes with a polished glass-morphism UI, fluid animations, and haptic feedback. Your app, your look.
