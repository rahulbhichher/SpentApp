FAQ

Common questions.

Is Spent really free?
Yes, Spent is completely free. The only thing premium does is remove ads — all features are available to every user at no cost.
What happens if I delete the app?
Since all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will permanently delete your data. We recommend exporting your transactions as CSV or PDF from Settings before uninstalling.
Does Spent work offline?
Yes, 100%. Spent is fully offline — all your data is stored locally on your device and never uploaded to any server. The only network activity is for displaying ads (free version) and fetching currency exchange rates.
How does the AI work without internet?
All AI insights (spending personality, pattern detection, monthly stories) are powered by on-device algorithms. No data is sent to any server — the analysis happens entirely on your phone using your local transaction history.
Is my receipt data safe?
Absolutely. Receipt scanning uses on-device OCR (Google ML Kit). Your photos are processed locally, never uploaded, and are not stored permanently — only the extracted transaction details are saved after you approve them.
