Features

Everything you need to manage money

Powerful features, zero data collection.

🔒

100% Offline & Private

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

📚

Multi-Account Passbooks

Manage Cash, Bank, Savings, and custom accounts with individual balances and colors.

⚡

Smart Transaction Entry

Quick-add expenses, income, or transfers with auto-focused amount fields and clipboard extraction.

📋

Paste & Log

Copy a bank SMS or UPI confirmation. Spent extracts transaction details for one-tap approval — all locally.

🧠

Merchant Learning Engine

Automatically maps merchant names and remembers your category choices over time.

🛡

Duplicate Prevention

SHA-256 fingerprinting prevents the same transaction from being logged twice.

🎨

Custom Categories

Build hierarchical category trees with 24+ icons and 15 color options for income and expenses.

🔄

Subscription Tracker

Log recurring subscriptions with billing cycles and see your true monthly burn rate.

💱

Currency Converter

Real-time exchange rates for 10+ currencies with offline caching and 3-tier fallback.

💡

Spending Personality

Rule-based analytics classifies you as Saver, Balanced, Spender, or Impulse Buyer with actionable advice.

🔍

Pattern Detection

Identifies behaviors like Weekend Splurge, Late-Night Shopping, and Subscription Trap.

🎞

Monthly Stories

Instagram-style animated slideshows recapping your financial month with personality-driven narratives.

📈

Reports & Export

Visual bar charts, date range filtering, and export to CSV, PDF, or chart screenshot.

🔓

Biometric Lock

Secure your data with Face ID, Touch ID, or Fingerprint authentication.

🌈

Theming

Light, Dark, and System-auto themes with glass-morphism UI design.

🔔

Notifications & Reminders

Daily reminders, weekly insights, subscription reminders, and spending alerts.
